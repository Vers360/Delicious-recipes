Unicorn Dip

Description

A beautiful rainbow unicorn dip might be the most magical recipe you'll ever make!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cream cheese or vegan cream cheese
  • 1 cup yogurt of choice such as coconutmilk yogurt
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sugar of choice or erythritol for sugar-free
  • 1 additional tbsp sugar or pinch uncut stevia
  • food coloring OR a pinch turmeric acai powder, spirulina, and blue spirulina

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients except the colors until smooth.
  2. Divide among four little dishes and stir a different coloring into each dish.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of each color into a serving bowl, alternating colors.
  4. Then swirl colors together with a spoon.
